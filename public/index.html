suburban dogs

"Suburban Dogs" is Northwest's second full-length album, released on November 15th, 2019. The album features 12 songs, all of which were produced entirely by Lukas Needham and Noah Bennett, with the occasional help of drummer Blake Fletcher and bassist Hayden Carlson. Throughout the record there are recurring motifs of love, nostalgia, friendship, as well as depression, anxiety, and regret. These themes manifest as the album progresses, as it loosely describes a plot of two lovers meeting, connecting, then falling out. The first track, "New Feel", was finished and released by January of 2019.

area code

Northwest's debut album, "Area Code", was released on July 15th of 2018. The album is comprised of 11 songs, all produced by best friends Lukas Needham and Noah Bennett. The album was originally meant to include a long overarching story, complete with a myriad of skits, interludes, and dialogue, but the idea was scrapped early on in the creation of the album. Despite the removal of much of the narrative content, the record's general premise remains a collection of thoughts, feelings, and inquiries into friendship, love, and self-image. After the album's inception, response by friends and peers was generally positive, and consequently lead to talk of live Northwest shows. Thus, Northwest added drummer Blake Fletcher -- a childhood friend of Lukas -- and bassist Hayden Carlson -- a cousin and childhood friend of Noah.
